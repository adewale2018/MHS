ABOUT MHS

Having observed the presence of many private Islamic nursery and primary schools in Muslim-Ifelodun Area with just only three private Islamic secondary schools as at then, Muflihun High School was established in a serene environment of Muslim-Ifelodun in September, 2014 as a day school. The school started with JSS1, JSS2 and SSS1 classes with a total number of forty-seven (47) students for 2014/2015 academic session. Read More
